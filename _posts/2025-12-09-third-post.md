In the Movie I Robot  A detective from Chicago,also known as Will Smith is under a investigation involoving a Robotic pioneer sucide case.This case  leads to a conspiracy which challenges the Three laws of Robotics in the movie which were 1. Robots can't harm humans or allow harm through inaction; 2. They must obey human orders unless they conflict with the First Law; and 3. They must protect their own existence as long as it doesn't conflict with the First or Second Laws.However in the begining of the movie Robots are helping humans in good beneficial ways,Then later new robots are created that  threatens society and  humanity.
